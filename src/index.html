缘分 (Yuánfèn) A destined meeting

China — golden courtyards, silk-road myths, roofs that refuse gravity

Wander forbidden gardens, painted eaves, and stories older than the maps that tried to hold them.

I’m Marina Budarina

I’m trying to make the internet fun again. This was inspired by the beaded doorway curtains we always had at home.

I’m from Kazakhstan, and living as a nomad, every country feels like another door.

So I gave each one its own curtain — woven from its architecture, its wisdom, its language, its sound.

A small interactive way to experience a culture beyond just photos.
